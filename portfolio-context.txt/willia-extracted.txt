Introduction
IMPORTANCE OF INTERNSHIP
On-the-Job Training (OJT) is an essential part of the institutionâ€™s curriculum that allows students to gain exposure to different fields of work in society. It serves as a bridge that prepares students for their future careers. OJT is a form of skills development where students learn by actively participating in real work environments. Through hands-on experience, students are given the opportunity to apply the theories and concepts they have learned in school. This training not only enhances their practical knowledge but also helps them develop important skills needed in the workplace. Furthermore, it allows students to gain confidence and become more familiar with professional responsibilities.
OBJECTIVES OF THE INTERNSHIP
On-the-Job Training plays a vital role in educating students about their chosen career paths while also introducing them to the realities of the working world. It exposes students to actual tasks and responsibilities related to their field of study. This experience serves as a stepping stone in gaining valuable knowledge and practical experience that will be beneficial in their future careers. OJT helps students develop competence and improves their ability to perform tasks efficiently and effectively. In addition, it enhances their professionalism and teaches them how to interact with different types of people in the workplace. After completing the training, students are expected to handle various situations appropriately and communicate well with colleagues, supervisors, and clients.
TIME AND PLACE OF THE INTERNSHIP
On-the-Job Training requires dedication, effort, and a serious attitude. Student trainees must take this opportunity responsibly, as it plays a significant role in preparing them for their future as professionals. It can also serve as a valuable source of recommendations when they transition from being students to entering the workforce.
Having a well-structured OJT program helps in shaping future professionals by equipping them with the necessary skills and experiences. Even the simplest tasks learned during training can be applied in real job situations. This process gradually molds students into competent and competitive individuals who are ready to face the demands of their chosen careers. Ultimately, OJT is not just a requirement, but a meaningful experience that contributes to personal growth and professional development
